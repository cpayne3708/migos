MIGOS

Quavo

Quavious Keyate Marshall (born April 2, 1991), known professionally as Quavo, is an American hip hop recording artist from Lawrenceville, Georgia. He is best known as a member of the hip hop and trap music trio Migos. The members of the Migos are all directly related. Quavo is Takeoff's uncle and Offset is Quavo's cousin.
Outside of Migos, Quavo has been featured on four singles which have peaked within the top 10 of the Billboard Hot 100.

Takeoff

Better known by his stage name Takeoff, Kirshnik Ball is a part of the hip hop trio called Migos whose 2013 hit "Versace" went viral and peaked at #99 on the Billboard Hot 100. He started making beats and developing rhythms in the seventh grade, but didn't start taking music seriously until 2011.

Offset

Kiari Kendrell Cephus (born December 14, 1991), known professionally as Offset, is an American hip hop recording artist from Lawrenceville, Georgia. He is best known as a member of the hip hop and trap music trio Migos, alongside his family members Takeoff and Quavo.
